# STRIKE GEN AI — Privacy Policy

Version: 0.1

Effective Date: 2026-07-09

Author: STRIKE GEN AI Legal & Product Team

---

## 1. Overview

This privacy policy describes how STRIKE GEN AI collects, uses, stores, and shares personal information. It is a planning-stage document aligned with the [Security Architecture](security-architecture.md) and will be finalized with legal counsel before launch.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### Account information
- Email address, password (hashed), display name, avatar.
- Account status and role.

### Usage information
- Generation requests (prompts, parameters), generated assets, project metadata.
- Credit and subscription history, payment metadata (not raw card data).

### Operational information
- IP address, user agent, device/session metadata.
- Activity logs and audit entries.

### Information from third parties
- Identity verification and payment metadata from our providers.

We do **not** collect or store raw payment card numbers. Payment data is tokenized by our payment processor.

---

## 3. How We Use Information

- To provide the service: authentication, generation, asset storage, billing.
- To communicate: notifications, receipts, support responses.
- To operate and protect the platform: monitoring, abuse detection, security, audit.
- To improve the product: aggregated analytics on usage and quality.
- To comply with legal obligations.

We do **not** sell personal information to third parties.

---

## 4. Lawful Basis (GDPR-aligned)

Where applicable, we process personal data on the following bases:
- **Contract** — providing the service the user signed up for.
- **Legitimate interest** — platform security, abuse prevention, and product improvement.
- **Consent** — optional marketing communications (withdrawable anytime).
- **Legal obligation** — financial record retention and law enforcement responses.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We share data with:
- **AI providers** — prompts and parameters necessary to generate requested media.
- **Payment processor** — billing metadata required for transactions.
- **Email provider** — email addresses and message content for transactional email.
- **Cloud infrastructure providers** — storage and compute hosting.

All providers are bound by data processing agreements. We do not share data for cross-context advertising.

We disclose data to authorities only when legally required and narrowly scoped.

---

## 6. Data Retention

| Data | Retention |
|---|---|
| Account records | While the account is active; anonymized after deletion within 30–90 days |
| Financial records | 7 years (legal requirement) |
| Generated assets | Per plan retention; user-configurable archival |
| Activity logs | 90 days hot, up to 1 year archived |
| Audit logs | 7 years |

Users may request early deletion of assets subject to legal hold exceptions.

---

## 7. User Rights

Depending on the user's jurisdiction, they may have the right to:
- **Access** their personal data.
- **Correct** inaccurate data.
- **Delete** their account and associated data (subject to financial retention).
- **Export** their data in a portable format.
- **Object** to processing based on legitimate interest.
- **Withdraw consent** for optional processing.
- **Lodge a complaint** with their supervisory authority.

Requests are submitted via support and fulfilled within 30 days. Identity verification is required before disclosing or modifying data.

---

## 8. Security

We protect personal data with the controls described in the [Security Architecture](security-architecture.md):
- TLS in transit, encryption at rest for sensitive data.
- Least-privilege access and RBAC.
- Immutable audit logs for sensitive actions.
- Regular security testing and incident response readiness.

---

## 9. International Transfers

Data may be processed in regions other than the user's home country. Where required, we rely on standard contractual clauses or equivalent transfer mechanisms, and we select providers with adequate privacy commitments.

---

## 10. Children's Privacy

The service is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their data. Accounts discovered to belong to minors are removed.

---

## 11. Cookies and Tracking

- Essential cookies for authentication and session management.
- Analytics cookies (optional, consent-gated) for product improvement.
- No advertising cookies.

Users can manage cookie preferences in the UI.

---

## 12. Changes to This Policy

Material changes are notified to users by email at least 30 days before taking effect. The revision history is maintained below.

---

## 13. Contact

Privacy questions and requests can be directed to the privacy contact via the support channel. Formal requests are handled within the timelines above.
