# STRIKE GEN AI — Branding Guidelines

Version: 0.1

Date: 2026-07-09

Author: STRIKE GEN AI Design Team

---

## 1. Brand Overview

STRIKE GEN AI is a creator-focused AI platform. The brand should communicate professional creative power, speed, and accessibility.

Brand attributes:
- Professional
- Creative
- Approachable
- Trustworthy
- Forward-looking

---

## 2. Brand Voice & Tone

- **Voice:** Helpful, concise, encouraging.
- **Tone:** Professional but not stiff; confident but not arrogant.
- **Principles:**
  - Lead with verbs in CTAs ("Generate", "Download", "Save").
  - Keep microcopy short and action-oriented.
  - Explain costs and credits clearly; never hide pricing.
  - Acknowledge errors honestly and offer next steps.

---

## 3. Logo Usage

- **Primary logo:** horizontal wordmark + mark, used on light backgrounds.
- **Stacked variant:** for compact or square contexts.
- **Icon mark:** simplified mark for favicons and app icons.
- **Clear space:** maintain padding equal to the mark height on all sides.
- **Don'ts:** do not distort, recolor beyond approved palette, rotate, or add effects.

---

## 4. Color Palette

Primary palette (semantic roles, not raw hex — tokens defined in the design system):
- **Primary (Brand):** strong accent for primary actions and highlights.
- **Secondary:** complementary accent for secondary actions.

Neutral palette:
- **Surface:** light and dark background variants.
- **Border / Divider.**
- **Text:** primary, secondary, muted.

Semantic colors:
- **Success** — positive states.
- **Warning** — cautions.
- **Danger** — errors and destructive actions.
- **Info** — system notices.

Accessibility:
- Minimum 4.5:1 contrast for body text; 3:1 for large text.
- Color is never the sole carrier of meaning — pair with icons or text labels.

See [UI/UX Design System](ui-ux-design-system.md) for token definitions.

---

## 5. Typography

- **Primary typeface:** modern humanist sans-serif (chosen at implementation).
- **Scale:** tokenized sizes for H1–H6, body, small, caption.
- **Weights:** maximum three weights (regular, medium, bold).
- **Line height:** 150% for body, 120% for headings.

Rules:
- Maintain clear hierarchy and consistent spacing.
- Avoid all-caps for body; reserve uppercase for compact labels sparingly.

---

## 6. Imagery

- Use real creator examples and generated asset previews in the product.
- Marketing may use minimal abstract art that reflects creative motion.
- Avoid stock-photo clichés that feel disconnected from the creator audience.
- All imagery must have appropriate licensing and alt text.

---

## 7. Iconography

- Geometric, consistent stroke weight, simple forms.
- Provide filled and outline variants for different states.
- Icons support meaning; they never replace labels.
- Interactive icons require accessible labels (`aria-label`).

---

## 8. Logo and Brand Don'ts

- Do not recolor the logo outside the approved palette.
- Do not place the logo on low-contrast or busy backgrounds.
- Do not stretch, skew, or rotate the logo.
- Do not combine the logo with third-party brand marks without approval.

---

## 9. Naming Conventions

- Product name: **STRIKE GEN AI** (always capitalized in marketing).
- Short form: **Strike Gen** (acceptable in informal contexts).
- Feature names: title case in UI, lowercase in code identifiers.

---

## 10. Brand Application

- **Website:** primary logo in header, wordmark in footer.
- **Product UI:** simplified mark for avatars and loading states.
- **Email:** primary logo in header; plain-text fallback required.
- **Social:** square profile images use the icon mark.
