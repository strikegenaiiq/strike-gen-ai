# STRIKE GEN AI — Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Product Overview

STRIKE GEN AI is an AI-powered creator platform designed to help individuals, creators, agencies, and businesses generate professional digital content using simple prompts.

The platform combines AI video generation, image creation, audio generation, content management, subscriptions, and creator workspace tools into one unified ecosystem.

---

# 2. Vision

To democratize AI-powered content creation by providing accessible, scalable, and intelligent tools that allow anyone to transform ideas into professional-quality digital assets.

---

# 3. Mission

Build a creator-focused AI platform that simplifies content production through automation, intelligent workflows, and affordable access to advanced creative technologies.

---

# 4. Problem Statement

Content creators and businesses face challenges:

- High production costs
- Limited access to professional creative tools
- Time-consuming content production workflows
- Multiple disconnected AI tools
- Difficulty managing generated assets

STRIKE GEN AI aims to solve these challenges through an integrated AI creation workspace.

---

# 5. Target Users

## Individual Creators

Needs:
- Generate videos quickly
- Create social media content
- Reduce production time

## Marketing Teams

Needs:
- Produce advertising materials
- Generate campaign assets
- Improve workflow efficiency

## Creative Agencies

Needs:
- Manage multiple projects
- Produce client content
- Scale creative output

## Enterprises

Needs:
- Large-scale content generation
- Team collaboration
- Usage management

---

# 6. Core Product Goals

The platform should:

- Enable AI-powered content generation
- Provide a simple creator workflow
- Support subscription-based access
- Manage usage through credits
- Store and organize generated assets
- Provide administrative controls

---

# 7. MVP Features

## Authentication

Users should be able to:

- Create an account
- Sign in securely
- Manage profiles
- Access personalized dashboards

---

## AI Creator Workspace

Users should be able to:

- Create AI videos from prompts
- Generate images
- Generate audio content
- Save projects
- Manage creative assets

---

## Credit System

The platform should include:

- Usage-based credits
- Credit tracking
- Subscription allocation
- Usage history

---

## Subscription System

Users should have access to:

- Free plan
- Paid creator plans
- Upgrade options
- Billing management

---

## Admin Dashboard

Administrators should manage:

- Users
- Subscriptions
- Credits
- Platform analytics
- Revenue information

---

# 8. User Journey

Basic workflow:

Visitor ↓ Create Account ↓ Dashboard ↓ Create Content ↓ Spend Credits ↓ Save Project ↓ Upgrade Subscription

---

# 9. Success Metrics

The platform success will be measured by:

- Number of registered users
- Active creators
- Generated content volume
- Subscription conversions
- User retention
- Platform usage
