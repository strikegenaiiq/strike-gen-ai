# STRIKE GEN AI — Affiliate Program

Version: 0.1

Date: 2026-07-09

Author: STRIKE GEN AI Growth Team

---

## 1. Overview

The affiliate program lets external partners (creators, educators, agencies) earn commissions for referring paying customers to STRIKE GEN AI. Unlike the [Referral System](referral-system.md), this is a formal program for partners who drive volume. This is a planning-stage document.

See also:
- [Business Model](business-model.md) §12 — partnerships.
- [Revenue & Cost Analysis](revenue-and-cost-analysis.md) — commission budgeting.

---

## 2. Goals

- Expand reach via trusted partners with relevant audiences.
- Pay for verified, retained revenue rather than raw clicks.
- Keep the program economically sustainable.

---

## 3. Eligibility

- **Affiliate:** Applies and is approved; must comply with the [Terms of Service](terms-of-service.md) and branding guidelines.
- **Referred customer:** New paying customer attributed via the affiliate's link or code at signup.
- **Qualifying revenue:** First 12 months of the referred customer's paid subscription and credit purchases.

---

## 4. Commission Structure

| Tier | Qualifying revenue (per referred customer) | Commission |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | First 12 months | 20% |
| Partner (high volume) | First 12 months | 30% |
| Enterprise (negotiated) | Per contract | Custom |

- Commissions are recurring on the customer's payments for the 12-month window.
- Refunded or chargebacked transactions claw back the commission.
- Commissions are paid out monthly once a minimum threshold (e.g., $50) is reached.

---

## 5. Attribution

- Affiliate links carry a partner ID captured at signup.
- Attribution lasts for 90 days from first click (cookie + server-side record).
- Direct signups without attribution do not earn commission.
- Disputes over attribution are resolved using server-side records; cookie-only claims are not honored.

---

## 6. Fraud Controls

- Self-referrals and referrals within the same household/organization are void.
- Brand bidding on search ads for STRIKE GEN AI branded terms is prohibited.
- Coupon-site or cashback-site attribution is not paid unless under a separate agreement.
- Unusual conversion patterns are flagged for manual review before payout.

---

## 7. Payouts

- Payouts are made via the affiliate's chosen method (bank transfer, PayPal).
- Payouts require a minimum accumulated balance and a valid invoice/W-9 where applicable.
- Tax documentation is collected per jurisdiction before first payout.
- Payouts are delayed by a 30-day holding period to accommodate chargebacks.

---

## 8. Affiliate Dashboard

- Partner signup and application.
- Personalized referral link and promo code.
- Conversions, qualifying revenue, and pending payouts.
- Marketing assets (logos, banners) per the [Branding](branding.md) guidelines.

---

## 9. Admin Oversight

- Affiliate approval and suspension.
- Payout approval workflow with finance review.
- Fraud queue and commission adjustment tools.
- Aggregate affiliate metrics in [Analytics & Reporting](analytics-and-reporting.md).

---

## 10. Future Considerations

- **Co-marketing campaigns** with top partners.
